About Us

Who Are We?

Our team is comprised of passionate members from the Society of Women Engineers (SWE) at the University of Texas at Austin, each dedicated to empowering students through free, high-quality educational resources. We are driven by a shared commitment to improving STEM education and making a positive impact on the learning experiences of middle and high school students.
